✶ Calculation Overview
Offer Cost: The total monetary value of the offer to the customer (e.g., $25 total savings). It represents the brand's opportunity cost.
Example: If the monthly cost of a subscription is $19.99 and the offer is "2.99 for 3 months", the calculation for Offer Cost is: Discount Amount × Promotion Period = ($19.99–2.99) ×3 months = $51
